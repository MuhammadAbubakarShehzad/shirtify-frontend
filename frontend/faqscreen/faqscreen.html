Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about orders, design studio features, shipping, and account help.

Orders and Products

How do I place an order?

Browse the shop, choose a shirt, add it to cart, and complete checkout with your shipping details.

Can I customize my own shirt?

Yes. Open Design Studio, select a shirt, and use the editor to add text, shapes, drawings, and images.

Can I save my design?

Use the Save button inside Design Studio to keep your work before adding it to cart.

Shipping and Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on your location, but you can expect your order status to update after payment confirmation.

How can I track my order?

Use the order information page after checkout to check your purchase details and order status.
