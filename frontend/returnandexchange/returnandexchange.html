Returns & Exchange Policy

We keep the process simple. Check the policy below before starting a return or exchange request.

01

Return Window

Items can be requested for return within 7 days of delivery as long as they are unused, unwashed, and in original condition.

02

Exchange Eligibility

We accept exchanges for size issues, damaged items, or wrong product deliveries. Exchange requests are reviewed before approval.

03

How to Request

Email shirtify@gmail.com with your order number, reason for the request, and a clear photo if the item arrived damaged.

04

Refund Timing

Approved refunds are processed after inspection. The final credit timeline depends on your payment method and bank processing time.
